Lasagna

A hearty, layersome dish with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese—perfect for family dinners or meal prep!

Ingredients

For the Meat sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • large onion, finely diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (85% lean)
  • 1 lb (450g) Italian sausage, casings removed (mild or spicy)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and sauté until soft, 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef and sausage. Break up the meat with a spoon and cook until browned, 8-10 minutes. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1 minute to caramelize.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, bay leaves, oregano, basil, thyme, salt, pepper, red wine (if using), and beef broth. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low. Cover partially and cook for 1.5-2 hours, stirring occasionally. This slow cook deepens the flavor. Remove bay leaves before using.
